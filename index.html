Did You Know?

Interesting facts about our destinations

🍷
World's oldest winery — The Areni-1 cave holds the oldest winemaking facility on Earth, dating back 6,100 years.
⛪
First Christian nation — Armenia became the world's first country to adopt Christianity as its state religion in 301 AD.
🏔️
Biblical Mount Ararat — The legendary resting place of Noah's Ark is visible from Yerevan on clear days.
📜
Unique alphabet — The Armenian script, created in 405 AD, is one of the world's most distinctive writing systems still in use today.
🌊
Lake Sevan — One of the world's largest freshwater high-altitude lakes, sitting at 1,900 metres above sea level.

Top Activities in Armenia

Year-round adventures from snowy ski slopes to blooming summer trails and harvest vineyard tours.

⛷️
Skiing & Snowboarding
Tsakhkadzor resort offers world-class slopes from December to March with panoramic views.
🥾
Trekking & Hiking
The Transcaucasian Trail passes through gorges, forests, and traditional mountain villages.
🏺
Pottery & Crafts
Try traditional Armenian carpet weaving and ceramic arts in the historic city of Gyumri.

Duduk Music Workshops
Learn the UNESCO-listed duduk wooden flute from master musicians in Yerevan.

Culinary Tours
